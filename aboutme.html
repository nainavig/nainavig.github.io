About Me

I'm currently a senior at Wesleyan University in Middletown, CT. I grew up in New Hampshire with my two older sisters and many pets. From a young age I was fascinated by math, so at Wesleyan I dove right in and decided to be a mathematics and computer science double major. At Wesleyan I spent time focusing on computer networks in particular, which was the topic of my research this summer. I also spent two terms at Dartmouth College this past spring as an exchange student, where I took a couple of interesting electives including Artificial Intelligence and Software Design and Implementation. I'm currently seeking roles in Software Engineering and Full Stack Development. In my free time I enjoy knitting, cooking, and throwing pottery.
